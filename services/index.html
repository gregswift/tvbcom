Services

Today's Vision on Bulverde offers thorough, complete eye examinations to uncover any visual and/ or medical ocular complications. Our services include:

  • fitting for routine, toric, multifocal, colored, and medically necessary contacts lenses
  • diabetic eye disease assessment and reporting to your endocrinologist
  • cataract evaluation
  • dry eye management
  • glaucoma evaluation and management
  • amblyopia (lazy eye) evaluation and management
  • treatment of stye, conjunctivitis, and other "pink eye" type infections
  • treatment of eye injuries such as a foreign body or corneal abrasion
  • pediatric vision evaluations - we are Infantsee providers
  • refractive surgery co-management
  • high risk medication monitoring
  • visual field testing
